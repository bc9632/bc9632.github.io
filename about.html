Who we are

And what we do...

#bakeforcharity


We are a small group of volunteers based in Batley, West Yorkshire, who dedicate our spare time to creating delicious sweet treats for you to eat! All of our profits are donated to One Nation - a local charity who have numerous projects both in the UK and around the world, working to improve and save lives every day.


In just 4 weeks during 2019, we raised an incredible Â£1,370.62 that went towards rebuilding lives in Palestine, by providing food, water and medical supplies to those most in need.




Now we're back, and we're bigger than ever. Together, we think we can continue to change lives all over the world. And what's your role in this? It's pretty simple... we just need you to eat cake!



How it works:


1. Place your order on our website for your chosen collection date.

2. Your order will be confirmed, and you will be given an Order Number.

3. Payment is accepted by cash upon collection.

4. Collect your baking - and enjoy!
