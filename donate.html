How your donations are used...

One Nation has a 100% donations policy - that means anything you give to them goes directly to the causes they're intended for. Any administration costs are covered by donations made specifically for that purpose, or recycled clothing.

Their projects are being carried out both in the UK and in countries across the world. Anything donated through our website will be forwarded onto them for their 'Most Needy' causes. This helps to provide funding for emergency situations as and when they arise.

If you would like to make a more specific donation than this, you can head over to their website and choose exactly which project you would like it to go towards.
